External Results

External Elections for the 26th Management Committee and 45th NUSSU EXCO Representative was held from 28 to 30 August 2023.

Total valid votes casted refer to the sum of all For and Against votes.

% For votes were calculated by taking the number of For votes over the Total valid votes casted. Abstained votes were not included in the calculation.

For Vote of Confidence, candidates who obtained at least 50% of For votes will be deemed to pass the External Elections.

Management Committee Candidates

There were 11 candidates running for the External Elections. As such, a Vote of Confidence was conducted for the Management Committee Elections.

Elected members will proceed to the Internal Elections, where the roles will be decided.
